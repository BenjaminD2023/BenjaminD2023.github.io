Chapter 4

DM read the following:

You are all at Tokugawa Ieyasu's base. Tokugawa Ieyasu sits in front of you, he asks you guys about the battle plan.

expected response from players: We didn't find the battle plan/Hand Tokugawa Ieyasu the paper they found at room #8 of the basement.

If the players handed Tokugawa the paper they found, Tokugawa will tell them Toyotomi's attack against Korea, has already started, since he's already died, Hideyori will probably be in charge. His Cavalry will come out first, followed by the samurai, then the firearms. They will all attack from the 2 sides. He's hoping he can break through and defeat Korea. He's planning on a mass murder in Korea and annexing Korea. Don't forget, if you want to beat Toyotomi, come to Seikigahara tomorrow morning for the battle of Seikigahara. We'll attack tomorrow.

Everyone goes to bed and can take a long rest now

The next morning, you arrived at Sekigahara, a little town located between Maibara city and Tarui town.

You found yourself arriving early, you can go investigate the town:

  • Go eat in the town (Gains 20 Temp HP)
  • Go ask about Toyotomi's Army around the town. Result: Toyotomi's Army will arrive in 1d4-1 days
  • Go report to Tokugawa Ieyasu (Must)
  • They can seek Toyotomi's Army already at the town if they succeed on a passive Perception.

After they completed 1d4 of the above, they will hear sound of the cavalry, they have to run to Tokugawa's Army and prepare for the fight. [Roll Initiative, the fight begins]

The players can see Toyotomi's army in 1d4 rounds, before that, they can set up the battle field however they want.

After they can see the army, Toyotomi will force them to get to Hideyori and attempt to kill him. Roll Stealth (DEX) every round. DC 15+X (X is the amount of rounds, total 5 rounds)

  • Fail in 1st round: Get hit by a Light Cavalry
  • Fail in 2nd round: Get hit by a Heavy Cavalry
  • Fail in 3rd Round: Get hit by a Samurai
  • Fail in 4th round: Get fired at by a 2nd Level Ashigaru firearm
  • Fail in 5th round: Get fired at by a 3rd Level Ashigaru firearm

After they reached Hideyori, they will fight against Hideyori. Roll for Encounter after each Hideyori's round

d6 Encounters
1 A cannon fired at the position and caused an explosion in the battlefield. Everyone in the battlefield must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw DC 19 or take 5d6 explosion damage.
2 3 Lv.2 Ashigaru (Firearm)
3 2 Samurai
4 Arrows comes from different sides of the battle field, everyone (Including Hideyori) has to succeed on a DEX 17 Saving throw, or take 2d10 Piercing Damage
5 Nothing Happens
6 Nothing Happens

Players win if Hideyori gets defeated, lose if they all die

If they beat Toyotomi:

Tokugawa Ieyasu became the Shogun for Japan, and the invasion of Korea stops. He established a new shogunate capital at Edo, Japan is peaceful and stable. A new form of art called Hiroshige developed. It is made out of Woodblock, it can be printed for many times. This form of art is cheap, mostly everyone have one of these in their house. Also, a sport called Sumo is very popular, you can join a competition and play with others if you will later. A type of play called Kabuki is also popular in this period. You all gain 1000 GP, Congratulations in successfully helping is establishing a new shogunate. The Edo aka Tokugawa Shogunate. Japan's new golden age after 123 years of warfare!

If Toyotomi Defeated them all:

Even though it is peace inside Japan. (Toyotomi Hideyori killed everyone who have different ideas than him) Hideyori invaded Korea. He annexed Korea and killed a lot of Koreans. [Pointing to the Korean in this party], All of your family got killed. He also invaded China, even though he fails, but China is very weak now, and the Western powers started to sell Opium in China. Due to the weak government, the Opiums didn't get controlled, mostly everyone's addicted to Opium, there's Opium store everywhere, everyone became poor because they bought too much Opium. All of you guys also got killed by Hideyori.
