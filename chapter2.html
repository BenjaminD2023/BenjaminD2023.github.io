Chapter 2: Road to the Shogunate

  1. Part 1: The Way to Edo
    • Random Encounters (totally for fun 2333) roll d6
      1 An elderly couple is walking down the road. They are lost and looking for directions. They will offer to trade a magic item for directions to their destination, or they will trade information for food and shelter. (weapon: A +2 magical Longbow)
      2 Players come across a group of villagers. The villagers are in the middle of a celebration. They will ask the players to join in the celebration. (The players could decide to leave or stay)
      -If stay: The villagers happily welcome them and give them an Improved Matchlock gun (Simply add +2 to your Matchlock gun's damage)
      3 The party sees a wandering old Ashikaga samurai who is dancing and singing in the snow. He is lost, he is cold, and he is hungry. He will attack the party if they get too close to him! (no firearm Ashikaga samurai, exhaustion level 2)
      4 You see a man sitting on rock and staring at the castle. His name is Samuel. He will sell you his poems about: war, death and darkness for 2 silver pieces each, or his poems about: love, light and hope for 1 gold piece each. (DM could make up hilarious, nonsense poem just for fun LOL)
      5 A group of men are playing dice in an alleyway. If the players approach, they will stop what they’re doing, hide their dice, and act like they were just walking by. If the players choose to catch them and send them to the nearest guard, they will be awarded 50gp each and the trust of the guards (They will gain advantage in every roll to persuade the guards to help them or explain things in this city from now on.)
      6 A group of people are gathered around a woman named Margareta, who is telling them a story about a group of people who were stranded on an island, and then had to fight off a group of monsters who were attacking them. She is making up most of it as she goes along. She will try to stop the party to listen to her story.


      DM read the following: Toyotomi Hideyoshi is planning on invading Korea. He wants to turn the shame into glory because he failed in his first invasion of Korea. He wants all of you to guard Edo for him and protect all states around Edo away from other Daimyo’s invasion.
      Players can choose to investigate more in Toyotomi Hideyoshi’s camp or to start moving to Edo.
      1. If players decided to investigate in the camp, they must succeed on an INT (investigation) check. If they succeed, they will find a samurai knife (STR Weapon, 2d6 + 3 damage) and a chain mail. If they fail, they will get found by the guard and suspicious point + 2-5 (Based on their explanation on why they’re still in the camp. DM gets to decide the persuasion DC and the number of suspicious points to add).
      When the player heads off to Edo, DM will roll for random encounters according to the following table:
      Roll (d100) Level XP Award (Total) Monsters Quantity
      1~40 Easy 250 Rebellion Army’s Ashigaru (No Firearm) 5
      41~70 Medium Amount of Ashigaru they killed. (Each for 100XP, They can each make an attack on one of the running away Ashigaru when the Ashigaru run away) Rebellion Army’s Ashigaru Lv.1 (Firearm) 6 (rest will run away when 3 of them are defeated or captured)
      71~95 Null 0 No Encounter No Encounter
      96~100 Hard 500 Northern Barbarian Ashigaru (No Firearm) 10
      If players decided to capture a Rebellion Army’s soldier, they can try to ask about Rebellion Army’s next step. The soldier would tell the players the following if the player decided not to kill him and succeed on an Intimidation (CHA) check DC 15.
      The Rebellion Army’s still can’t have a good army rebuilt. His army is very weak now. (He looked at the players, noticed they’re from Toyotomi Clan) You can attempt to destroy his firearm storage now at night. You just got to make sure you don’t get caught by the weak guard. Even if you do, I think you can beat the guard easily.

      If the players decided to tell Toyotomi Hideyoshi this information before their next long rest, suspicious point –3 (minimum suspicious point: 0)

      Players are allowed to take away Ashigaru’s (Firearm)'s Matchlock Gun.


          Matchlock Gun requires a attack action to reload it after each shot. Player gets +2 bonus on To Hit rolls when attacking with it. The damage this gun deals on a hit is 2d6 + Wisdom Modifier Piercing Damage, it deals a extra 1d6 Fire damage to opponents wearing flammable armor.



      Afterwards, if the players succeed a Perception (WIS) check DC12, they can see a samurai spy running down the forest, screaming for help (He said: 'Please help me! I could tell you everything I know!'). He is being chased by several (3) rebellion soldiers. Players can either help him by fighting the Samurai, or they can run away and leave him to die. The Samurai is a Short bow Samurai -If they defeated the Samurai, they would get information from the man about where they could find the nearest way to the Gunpowder storage (The man said: "You guys are going to the rebeller'sunpowder storage, right?! I know the sequence of rebeller's patrol, you can avoid much fighting then.") -If they finish this task, when they go blow up the Gunpowder storage, they won't meet the guards of the cave (less fighting) in room 2,4, and 5, also, they won't meet the wandering guards. The DM will say: "because you all got the information from the spy, you all crept perfectly into the cave from the secret passage without having to fight the guard in the corridor"



      [DM should ask whether the party wants to explore the old fortress]
