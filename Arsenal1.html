                              1. 0~9: you didn’t remember anything of this so-called “Soza Samonji”  
                              
                              2. 10~16: you successfully remember “Soza Samonji” was a famous sword that was owned by several famous daimyos. Unfortunately, you only knew all the daimyos’ names who once owned this sword but didn’t remember the sequence:  
                              Imagawa Yoshimoto, Oda Nobunaga, Takeda Nobutora, Toyotomi Hideyoshi, and Tokugawa Ieyasu 
                              
                              3. 17~20: you successfully remember “Soza Samonji” was a famous sword that was owned by several famous daimyos, and you also remember the sequence that this sword was given to these daimyos

After Yoshimoto’s death at the Okehazama, the sword came into possession of Oda Nobunaga. After the incident of Honnoji, Toyotomi Hideyoshi recovered the sword, which he later gave to Tokugawa Ieyasu as a gift.
