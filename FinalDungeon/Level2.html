Room Features

This room is originally designed to be a small library. Ask the players to roll for Perception (WIS) DC 15.
If they succeed, DM read the following:
As you can see, the book are all over the place. However, you can sense that this is not a normal library. It stores some magical books that is enchanted and may help you during this adventure. In order to find which one is the enchanted magic book, you all have to rearrange the books on to the shelves first. According to the Japanese Hiragana.

Correct Solving for the Puzzle

Change the other 9 book's first hiragana into English pronounciation (The Tale of Genji can't be converted) --> Put the 9 books onto the shelves by alphabetical order of the English pronounciation --> The Tale of Genji will rise to the average height of the players, and the first player to touch it can attune the special copy of the book. .
