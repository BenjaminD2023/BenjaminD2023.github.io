BOSS FIGHT ROOM/td>  Toyotomi Hideyoshi is lying on his bed now, players can do a Stealth (DC18) to attempt to stay in the room without waking up Toyotomi Hideyoshi. If every player succeed, they will get advantage on inititave rolls. Toyotomi Hideyoshi wakes up immedietly after the rolls, and he will place Acereraki Denda's remain in front of the players. He will then tell the players, "Actually, Acereraki Denda betrayed all of you. He was working for me all the time, and now I know everything you're trying to do. Ha Ha Ha..." Then, the fight begins.

Encounters during the fight (d6, roll after the end of each Toyotomi's round):
d6 Encounters
1 3 Lv.1 Ashigaru (Firearm)
2 2 Lv.2 Ashigaru (Firearm)
3 2 Samurai
4 2 Light Cavalry
5 1 Lv.3 Ashigaru (Firearm)
6 Acereraki Denda will revive and help the players to fight Toyotomi Hideyoshi, if already revive, then reroll


After the Fight/td>   A bird will rise at the center of the room, "Figure Out a way to make the let the bird sing to get out of this basement". DM count 10 secs
Way to make the bird sing Result
Harming the Bird Fall into a pit and succeed on DEX DC21 Saving Throw or take 4d10 damage (Half on Succeed)
Use anything to make the bird sing Fall into Toyotomi's Tourture Device (2d10 per round inside, DC21 DEX Save to get out)
Wait for the Bird to sing (Do nothing in the 10 secs) A teleporter will rise in front of them, when they walk through, they will get tp out of the basement. Tokugawa is waiting for them outside. The end of Chapter 3, move on to Chapter 4
